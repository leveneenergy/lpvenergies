WHO WE ARE

The company is a Joint Venture (JV) enterprise between PriVida Limited (“PriVida”) and Levene Energy Development Limited(“Levene”).

LPV Energies leverages on latest technology, innovative design and seamless project execution to deliver top notch renewable energy solutions to our clients and customers.

Our Vision

Our vision is to generate 10,000 MW of electricity from renewable resources, focusing on Solar, Biomass, Wind and Hydrogen. The company is currently working with its partners to deliver a pipeline of projects valued at over $1.0bn dollars.

Our Mission

Our mission is to become a globally recognized business leader in the promotion, development and delivery of clean energy solutions particularly in developing economies of Africa.
