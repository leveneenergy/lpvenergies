Our Services

Building integrated photovoltaic (BIPV) Project

This service involves providing a renewable energy solution by installing integrated photovoltaic technology in buildings to generate power and also improve the overall aesthetic of the building. This solution aims to reduce energy cost to over 40% and increase the building energy efficiency and power stability. Currently LPV is installing one of a kind IPV renewable energy solution at the head office of a leading financial institution in Nigeria.

100MW Triplex Solar Panel Manufacturing Plant Project

This multimillion-dollar project will be the first 100MW capacity Solar PV manufacturing plant to be constructed in Nigeria which is expected to deliver 264,000 panels and 50,000 SHS kits annually.
Off-take agreements have been secured from key partners such as One-Watt Solar, Odua Investment Company (OICL), CESEL, and other sponsor owned projects. Combined offtake capacity is at c.160% of plant output.

2.8MW Solar Mini-Grid Project

The project is being conducted under the World Bank/REA Solar Naija program. It aims to deliver electricity connections to 22,520 households utilizing a hybrid Mini-grid solution.

LPV Energies has completed engagements with twenty-four (24) communities across Kogi, Benue and Nasarawa states. The company will adopt an Energy-As-A-Service (EAS) model and will deploy its proprietary Pay as You Go (PAYG) system for revenue collection.

Solar Home System (SHS) Project

The project is being conducted under the World Bank/REA Solar Naija program. We plan to deploy Pay As You Go (PAYG) Solar Home Systems to over 250,000 homes across Nigeria.

Over 228,000 units of SHS kits have been requested by different categories of customers, backed by letters of commitment and guarantees (Federal, State and Local Governments) and Off-Take Agreements (Distributors and Sales Agents).
