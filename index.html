How we work

LPV Energies deploys a World-Class Project Development Framework to ensure that its projects are bankable and attractive to investors globally.
1 - Site Identification
Identify potential sites and develop a rough technical concept at this stage..
2 - Pre-Feasibility
Analyse different available options and check technical feasibility and financial profitability
3 - Detailed Feasibility
Finalize assessment of the preferred technical option financially and secure all required permits, licences and permits to facilitate financing
4 - Finance & Contracting
Ensure each project is bankable start selecting key EPC and O & M contractors
5 - Detailed Design
Conduct a detailed design alongside finalizing our permitting process
6 - Construction

Construct the facility/plant whilst ensuring the highest level of quality and safety management

7 - Commissioning, Operations and Maintenance

Testing and initial commissioning of the plant precedes full operation of the plant
